Grading Systems

Gradesitory offers both 4 & 9 scale GPA calculations. Please review both the grading systems below and ensure you pick the correct one which is utilized at your educational institution.

The calculation algorithm is as follows:

  1. First your grades are converted to their corresponding grade point values using the conversion system shown below.
  2. Next the grade point value for each course is multiplied by its respective course credits/weight.
  3. Your GPA is the result of your total product of grade point value and course credit/weight divided by total credits/weight.
